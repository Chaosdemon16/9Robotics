My Dog

2900A

This is 2900A-A (Aka Anklebiter), The first attempt by my robotics team (Symbiosis A) to solve the problem presented by the VEX 2024-2025 game High Stakes.

Anklebiter is equipped with a 6 wheel 4 motor drivebase that is capable of operating at up to 200rpm. The back four wheels are omni-wheels that allow the robot to turn in place while the front wheels prevent other teams from pushing us sideways.

Anklebiter uses a two bar lift powered by two high tourqe motors to lift up a single motor claw mech that is the ideal size to grabs rings and dump them onto stakes, while also being tight enough while closed to lift mobile goals into corners during skills challenges.
